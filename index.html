Questions

Do I need to know how to use Framer? +
No. We build everything for you. If you already have a Framer account we work directly in it; if not, we set one up and hand it over on delivery. You receive the live site, the Framer project, and documentation on how to make copy and image changes yourself without touching code.
How long does a build take? +
Ignition delivers in 72 hours. Orbital in 5 business days. Universe in 10 to 14 days. Timeline starts from brand brief approval — we move fast because every hour your current site is live is an hour of lost conversion.
What do you need from me to start? +
A brand brief (colors, fonts, tone), your offer details (price, promise, audience), any existing copy or a rough idea of it, and your logo or wordmark. We handle everything else — layout, motion, code, mobile, and deployment.
Can you integrate with my existing payment system? +
Yes. We integrate with Stripe, PayPal, Hotmart, Kiwify, Eduzz, and most Latin American payment processors. For Orbital and Universe tiers we also build the embedded checkout overlay so there is zero friction at the moment of purchase.
What if I want changes after delivery? +
Ignition includes 2 revision rounds, Orbital includes 3, and Universe includes unlimited revisions for 14 days post-launch. After that, revision packs are available at a fixed rate. We stay close to our builds — we want them to convert as much as you do.
Is this only for Framer? +
The Spatial Engine is Framer-native — it is what makes the motion system and code overrides possible at this level. However we can adapt the visual and conversion architecture to Webflow for clients locked into that ecosystem. Reach out to discuss.
